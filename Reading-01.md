# Reading Assignment from class one. 

## 1. Headings
   - "#" denotes the biggest heading
   - "###" denotes the third biggest heading
   - "######" denotes the sixth biggest heading

## 2. Styling Text
   - ** ** for **bold**
   - _ _ for *italics*
   - ~~ ~~ for ~~strikethrough~~
      
## 3. Emojis! (remove spaces)
   - : +1 : gets you :+1:
   - : shipit : becomes :shipit:
